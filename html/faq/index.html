Here or some frequently asked questions. Hopefully, these will be helpful to you.

How do I purchase a Discord bot from your online shop?

You can create a ticket in our Discord server to begin the purchase of our bots in the #buy channel.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept Paypal payments. More payment methods available soon.

Can I customize the features of the Discord bot I'm purchasing?

You can request custom features for your Discord bot when you create a ticket for your purchase.

How long does it take to receive the Custom Discord bot after purchase?

Depends on the complexity of the features you require and how many orders we have, but you can expect only a few weeks for very complex projects.

Can I request additional features or modifications for the Discord bot?

When you are in the process of ordering the bot, you can request the additional features when testing the bot.

Do you offer customer support for the purchased Discord bot?

The main way we make our bots is with transparent communication with our customers. Our bot is only finished when our customer is satisfied with the product, and we take feedback to modify the bot to your liking.

Is there any refund policy if I'm not satisfied with the purchased Discord bot?

You won't have to purchase the bot if you're not satisfied with it. You'll be allowed to test the bot but won't get full access to it until you pay.

Can I use the Discord bot on multiple servers?

Yes, but only servers you own/are associated with. Sharing/selling the bot to other servers is not allowed and will result in the bot getting terminated.

Are there any recurring fees or additional costs associated with the purchased Discord bot?

No recurring fees, but there might be additional costs depending on the complexity of your request.

How frequently are the Discord bots updated with new features or bug fixes?

As soon as we find the newer version stable and ready for the public.

Can I transfer the ownership of the purchased Discord bot to someone else?

You are allowed to transfer ownership of the bot to another account, but not to someone else. You own the bot you purchased.

Are there any limitations or restrictions on the usage of the Discord bot?

Using any of our bots to break any Discord TOS and/or any laws is not allowed.

You didn't find what you were searching for?

You can contact us using the #support channel on our Discord Server
