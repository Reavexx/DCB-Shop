Why are our bots not free?

At our team, we are a group of dedicated students who are passionate about developing Discord bots as part of our projects. While we would love to offer our bots for free, it is important to understand that creating and maintaining these bots require a significant amount of time and effort.

By offering our bots at a reasonable price, we are able to cover these expenses and continue dedicating our time to enhance and provide support for the bots. This allows us to deliver a reliable and enjoyable experience to our users.

Thank you for understanding and considering our Discord bots. We value your support and are committed to delivering exceptional products and services to enhance your Discord experience.
